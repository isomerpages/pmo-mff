<p><strong>Subsidies for Preschool</strong>
<br><strong>Childcare and Infant Care Programmes</strong>
<br>Parents with Singapore Citizen children enrolled in licensed childcare
centres can receive a Basic Subsidy of up to $600 per month for full-day
infant care, and up to $300 per month for full-day childcare.</p>
<p>If you are a working mother with a gross monthly household income of $12,000
and below, you are eligible for the Additional Subsidy<strong>, </strong>on
top of the Basic Subsidy<strong>. </strong>Lower-income families will receive
higher subsidies.</p>

<p><strong>Kindergartens</strong>
<br>Singaporean children enrolled in an <strong>Anchor Operator or MOE Kindergarten</strong> are
eligible for the <a href="https://www.ecda.gov.sg/parents/subsidies-financial-assistance" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Kindergarten Fee Assistance Scheme (KiFAS)</u></a> if
the family’s gross monthly household income is $12,000 and below.</p>
<p>If you have three or more dependants, you can choose to have your income
assessed on a Per Capita Income basis for the Additional Subsidy or KiFAS
application.</p>

<h4><strong>FAQs</strong></h4>
<div data-type="detailGroup" class="isomer-accordion isomer-accordion-white">
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong>1. How do I apply for preschool subsidies?</strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p>All applications for the Basic and Additional Subsidy (for childcare and
infant care) and KiFAS (for kindergarten) should be made through your preschool
centre. The centre will provide you with the application form.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong>2. How will I receive the subsidies?</strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p>The subsidies will be disbursed directly to your preschool centre. You
will just need to pay the net fees after subsidy.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong>3. Is there additional help for larger families with many elderly or young dependents?</strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p>Yes, larger families (five or more members) with three or more dependents
can have their income assessed on a Per Capita Income basis for their Additional
Subsidy or KiFAS application.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong>4. Am I eligible for the Additional Subsidy if my child is enrolled in half-day and flexi-care programmes in childcare and infant care centres?</strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p>Yes. Subsidies for half-day and flexi-care programmes in childcare and
infant care centres are available. Details can be found on the Early Childhood
Development Agency’s website at <a href="https://www.ecda.gov.sg/parents/subsidies-financial-assistance#CCICS" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>https://www.ecda.gov.sg/parents/subsidies-financial-assistance#CCICS</u></a>
</p>
</div>
</details>
</div>
